About me

Hello, my name is Julia Nina Padilha, I hold a Post-Baccalaureate Diploma in Disaster Management at the Justice Institute of British Columbia, a Master’s degree in Psychology from Pontifícia Universidade Católica do Rio de Janeiro (PUC-Rio), and a Bachelor’s degree in Psychology from Universidade Federal do Maranhão (UFMA). My professional background includes experience in psychotherapy, higher education, academic research, community services, and Emergency Support Services (ESS) with the City of Vancouver. I am also certified in Psychological First Aid by the Canadian Red Cross.

My areas of expertise and interest include Emergency and Disaster Management, Humanitarian Assistance, Public Safety, Community Resilience, Psychosocial Support, Emergency Planning, Mental Health, Addictions, and Domestic Violence Prevention.
